EYES OF PHOTOGRAPHER

Rules

EOP competition rules and regulations. Read them carefully and prepare your images to reach the top.

1. GENERAL

-Anyone over 18 age can enter the contest.

-There are no date and time restrictions on when the image was taken.

-You are the unique owner and author of each photograph

-Presented image must be 100% photographic origin, this means using a camera or similar device, digital art images will not be accepted.

-Presented image cannot have been awarded in other contest

-Your image does not infringe copyrights, trademarks, contract rights or any other intellectual property rights of any third person or entity, neither violate the rights of privacy or publicity of any person.

2. IMAGE PREPARATION

-The files must keep the following specifications:

- sRGB or Adobe98 color space.

- The minimum dimension must be 2500 pixels on the long side.

- JPG format.

- No watermarks / Copyright units / Logos on images.

- Borderless photos only.

- Maximum size 100 megabytes.

3. COPYRIGHT AND RIGHTS OF USE

The copyright of the images will always belong to the photographer. Our sponsors do not acquire rights of use for commercial purposes.

For the winning images, the copyright holders allows contest organizer unlimited and non-exclusive use for activities in all media, including social media, online and printed, in relation with the contest and all promotional activities without any remuneration.
